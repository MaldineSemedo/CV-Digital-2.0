Who we are?

Institute for the promotion and development of innovation - CVDigital is a non-profit legal entity governed by private law, endowed with organizational, administrative, financial and patrimonial autonomy.

Main Objectives of CVDigital

Stimulate the practice and development of Information Systems and Technologies projects Encourage the practice of the highest quality of services for people, institutions and companies that use Information Technologies Foster a spirit of openness, creativity, innovation, credibility and experimentation in practice.

Our main mission

Promote the development of Information Systems and Technologies, ensuring the development of the scientific sector and encouraging the improvement of the critical spirit and professional responsibility of those who work in the Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics sector, in the Cape Verdean space.
